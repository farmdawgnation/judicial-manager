Judge

This name can be anything, but keep in mind an actual judge probably wouldn't enjoy being called Squidward, so it's probably best to use their actual name.

Judges will never judge teams from their organization. Please ensure you use the same spelling throughout. Spaces and capitalization will not be considered.
